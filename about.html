About Me

I am a sophomore astrophysics major at Rice University. I am currently involved in astroparticle research with Professor Christopher Tunnell and his research group. Previously, I have worked with Professor Edison Liang on high energy density nuclear research, helping him and his group run an experiment at the Texas Pettawatt laser.
