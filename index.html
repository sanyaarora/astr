Summary of Topics Covered in ASTR 408: Satistical Methods for Physics and Astronomy

This wesbite contains a summary of all of the topics covered in the Statistical Methods for Physics & Astronomy course at Rice University, taught by Prof. Patrick Hartigan. The reference text for this course is Modern Statistical Methods for Astronony (with R applications) by E. Feigelson and G. Babu. The course provides a general overview of many topics in statistics commonly used in data analysis, and therefore provides a good toolbox of analysis techniques that can later be explored further if needed.
